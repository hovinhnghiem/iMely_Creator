Reward Process
Social Content Creator

Period 1: 15/05/2026 - 15/07/2026

Step 1 - Apply

  • Fill out the application form with your personal information, TikTok channel link, 18+ confirmation, and bank account / MoMo payment information.
  • The team will review your application and send a confirmation email. Rewards count only for videos posted after you receive the confirmation email.

Step 2 - Post on TikTok

  • Create your video according to the program Content Guidelines.
  • You must use the hashtag #imely for the video to count.

Step 3 - Submit after 7 days

  • Exactly 7 days after posting, submit the Google Form with your TikTok video link and analytics screenshot showing views and likes.
  • The numbers at submission time are final and will not be updated later.
  • Submit before the 22nd of each month to receive payment that month. Submissions from the 23rd onward move to the next month.
Open Google Form →

Step 4 - Review

  • The team will respond within 2 business days by email with an approved or rejected status.
  • A maximum of 15 videos can be approved per creator across the full program.

Step 5 - Get paid

  • Payments are made once per month, on the 1st.
